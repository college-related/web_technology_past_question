                            URL vs URI
-URL = uniform resourse locator || -URI = uniform resourse indentifier
-It consists of location bar    || -It consists of location bar, name, address, etc


                                 HTTP vs FTP
-HTTP = hyper text transfer protocal    || -FTP = file transfer protocal
-It uses TCP port number 80             || -It uses TCP port number 20 and 21
-Used to transfer small files(webpage)  || -Used to transfer big files(upload, download)
-Authentication is not needed           || -Authentication is needed
